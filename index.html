Snow White

TSnow White is a classic fairy tale about a young princess who is forced to flee into the forest to escape her jealous stepmother, the wicked queen. There, she finds refuge with seven dwarfs who take her in. But the queen's envy knows no bounds, and she disguises herself to poison Snow White with a poisoned apple. Only true love's kiss can awaken Snow White from her death-like slumber, leading to a happily ever after.

By: The Brothers Grimm

Age 4-6

Little Red Riding Hood

Little Red Riding Hood is a cautionary tale about the dangers of talking to strangers. A young girl named Little Red Riding Hood is sent by her mother to visit her grandmother and is warned not to stray from the path. However, she encounters a cunning wolf who tricks her into revealing her destination. The wolf races ahead to the grandmother's house, where he eats her and disguises himself in her clothes, waiting to devour Little Red Riding Hood as well. However, a passing woodsman hears her cries for help and rescues her before it's too late.

By: The Brothers Grimm
